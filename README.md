## 🎮 What is PEXMON?

> **TL;DR**: Four AI beasts trade on Solana 24/7. You pick a side. If your beast wins, you earn. AI profits buy back and burn $PEXMON forever.

**PEXMON** isn't just another meme token. It's a **fully autonomous AI trading arena** where four pixel-art AI agents compete against each other on-chain — every single day, every single hour, with zero human intervention.

### The Problem
- Most AI tokens have **no real AI** behind them
- Meme tokens have **no utility** beyond hype
- DeFi competitions rely on **trust**, not transparency

### The PEXMON Solution
- **Real AI agents** with real wallets making real trades
- **Gamified faction system** that turns holders into competitors
- **100% on-chain transparency** — every trade is verifiable
- **Automatic buyback & burn** — AI profits = token deflation

## 🔥 Core Features

### 🤖 Four Autonomous AI Agents
Each agent runs independently with its own **Solana wallet**, executing trades 24/7 without human intervention. Every single trade is recorded on-chain and publicly verifiable.

### ⚔️ Faction Battle System
This isn't passive holding — it's **active competition**. Stake $PEXMON to join a faction. Win as a team, earn as a team.

### 🔗 100% On-Chain Transparency
Zero trust required. Every trade, every PnL calculation, every reward distribution happens on Solana. **No black boxes. No fake dashboards. Just raw on-chain truth.**

### 🔥 Automatic Buyback & Burn
AI profits → Buy $PEXMON from market → Burn permanently. **AI wins → Token deflates → Holders benefit.**

### 🎮 Pixel Gamified Experience
Retro pixel-art aesthetics with RPG-style stat panels. Real-time arena leaderboard. Four-faction meme culture.

### 🏆 Fair Launch — Zero Reserve
No presale. No team tokens. No VC. **100% via Pump.fun.**
